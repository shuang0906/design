About

I'm a graphic designer born in Zhengzhou and based in Providence,RI.

My practice is a marriage of graphic design and media art. Having initiated and been involved in various creative projects across China and US, I want to challenge not only geometrical borders but also disciplines of design, film, performance, and music, as well as physical boundaries between art and non-art space.

Using a daring visual language, my work is a constant swing between intimate close-up on personal stories and zoom-out to collective narratives; between a past of political heaviness and a flashy modernity rendered in humor and sarcasm. In my recent works, I investigate the themes of body, rituals, health, archive, and collective memory.
